Interesting Facts

Cats are nearsighted, but their peripheral vision and night vision are much better than that of humans. Male cats are more likely to be left-pawed, while female cats are more likely to be right-pawed.

Cats have an extra organ that allows them to taste scents on the air, which is why your cat stares at you with her mouth open from time to time. Cats are supposed to have 18 toes (five toes on each front paw; four toes on each back paw)

Cats’ claws all curve downward, which means that they can’t climb down trees head-first. Instead, they have to back down the trunk. .Cats can jump up to six times their length.
